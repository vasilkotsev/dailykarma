How it works

There are 1.5 milion charities in the U.S. Why pick just one?

Cause Funds allow you to drive more impact for all the charities you care about, while discovering new ones. Add your favorite nonprofits to your fund, pick a donation amount and you're done. Your secure donation will be divided evenly across all your organizations.
